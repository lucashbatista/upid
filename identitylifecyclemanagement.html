Identity Lifecycle Management (ILM)

Identity Lifecycle Management ensures the right people have the right access to the right systems-at the right time-automatically and securely.

What Identity Lifecycle Management Covers

  • Joiner - automatically provision access when employees, contractors, or partners onboard.
  • Mover - Adjust access as roles, departments, or responsabilities changes
  • Leaver - Promptly deprovision access when users exit the organization
  • Role & entitlement management - Ensure access aligns with job function and least&privilege access
  • Approval workflows - Enforce governance with audit-ready access requests and approvals
  • Integration - Connect HR systems, directories, SaaS apps, and on-prem systems

Why it matters

  • Reduces security risk by preventing orphaned and axcessive access
  • Improves compliance with SOX, SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and more
  • Increases operational efficiency through automation
  • Enhances user experience with faster onboarding and fewer access delays

Outcomes you can expect

  • Faster onboarding and offboarding
  • Fewer access-related incidents
  • Clearer visibility into who has access to what, and why
  • Scalable identity governance as your organizatiion grows
